# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the SMILE Loop community—including project maintainers, contributors, and users—will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Providing and accepting help generously
- Acknowledging and appreciating contributions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive language or jokes targeting individuals or groups
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Insulting or demeaning comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Dismissing or belittling others' perspectives or contributions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community interactions including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Project repositories and documentation
- Chat channels and forums
- Conferences, meetups, and events
- Email communications
- Social media interactions

This Code of Conduct applies to all community members, including:

- Project maintainers and core contributors
- Active contributors
- Occasional contributors
- Users and potential users
- Anyone representing the SMILE Loop project

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Contacting the maintainers** via email or GitHub
2. **Being specific** about what happened and who was involved
3. **Providing context** that helps us understand the situation
4. **Indicating impact** on you or the community

Reports will be:
- Treated confidentially
- Investigated promptly
- Handled with sensitivity

Retaliation against those who report violations is not tolerated.

### Contact

Report Code of Conduct violations to:
- **Email**: conduct@example.com
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/aaronbassett/SMILE/issues (with "Code of Conduct" label)
- **Direct Message**: Contact a maintainer privately

### Response

The SMILE Loop team will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt of your report within 48 hours
2. **Investigate** the situation thoroughly
3. **Determine** appropriate action (from warning to removal)
4. **Communicate** resolution to the reporter (if appropriate)
5. **Follow up** to ensure the issue is resolved

### Consequences

Depending on the severity and pattern of behavior, responses may include:

- **Verbal/written warning** - For minor first-time incidents
- **Temporary restriction** - Temporary loss of community privileges
- **Permanent removal** - For severe or repeated violations

## General Guidelines

### Being Inclusive

- Use inclusive language (avoid gendered terms unless appropriate)
- Consider that English may not be everyone's first language
- Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Help newcomers feel welcome

### Contributing Respectfully

- Assume good intentions in others' contributions
- Provide constructive feedback focused on ideas, not people
- Be open to learning from feedback
- Help others improve their work

### Handling Disagreement

- Disagree gracefully and respectfully
- Focus on the issue, not the person
- Take extended discussions to private channels if needed
- Accept majority decisions once made

## Support Resources

If you're struggling with the community or need support:

- **Community Discussions** - Ask questions and share experiences
- **Mentorship** - Experienced contributors can provide guidance
- **Mental Health** - Community members care about your wellbeing
- **Accessibility** - Let us know how we can better accommodate you
